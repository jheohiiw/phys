\section*{Problem 4: Charge $q_1$ for Equilibrium}

Given:
\[
Q = -3.1\,nC
\]

Distances:
\[
r_Q = 0.10\,m, \quad r_{q_1} = 0.20\,m
\]

For equilibrium of $q_2$:

\[
F_Q = F_{q_1}
\]

\[
k\frac{|Q|}{r_Q^2}
=
k\frac{|q_1|}{r_{q_1}^2}
\]

Cancel $k$:

\[
\frac{|Q|}{(0.10)^2}
=
\frac{|q_1|}{(0.20)^2}
\]

\[
\frac{3.1\times10^{-9}}{0.01}
=
\frac{|q_1|}{0.04}
\]

\[
3.1\times10^{-7}
=
\frac{|q_1|}{0.04}
\]

\[
|q_1| = 1.24\times10^{-8}\,C
\]

\[
|q_1| = 12.4\,nC
\]

\[
\boxed{q_1 \approx 12\,nC}
\]
