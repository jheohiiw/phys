\section*{Problem 7: Infinite Line Charge}

Choose a cylindrical Gaussian surface of radius $r$ and length $L$.

Flux through curved surface:

\[
\Phi_E = E(2\pi r L)
\]

Charge enclosed:

\[
Q_{enc} = \lambda L
\]

Gauss's Law:

\[
E(2\pi r L)
=
\frac{\lambda L}{\varepsilon_0}
\]

Cancel $L$:

\[
E(2\pi r)
=
\frac{\lambda}{\varepsilon_0}
\]

\[
\boxed{
E = \frac{\lambda}{2\pi\varepsilon_0 r}
}
\]
